What You’ll Learn

  • Get a wet look that lasts. Learn how to use Sealthane Wet Look, a 2-part, water-based acrylic urethane, to get a durable, high-gloss finish that enhances color, binds joint sand, and stands up to water, stains, and UV.
  • Reset bad or unknown sealers. See PSR Gel and Spray used as your “reset button” on failed or mystery sealers so you can safely remove built-up films and start fresh.
  • Run a repeatable strip–prep–seal system. Take home a clear workflow for dwell times, rinsing, surface moisture, sanding, and cure timing so you know not just how to seal, but when it’s safe to seal.
  • Price and position sealing as a real service. Learn how to quote jobs so sealing becomes a profitable add-on or maintenance program instead of a headache.

Who This Training Is For

This session is designed for people who actually live with the results of sealing work—not just the brochure.

  • Landscapers – who install or maintain pavers and concrete and want to add sealing as a high-margin add-on without callbacks when the sealer fails, turns white, or peels.
  • Hardscape contractors – who need a reliable way to strip off bad or unknown sealers, reset the surface, and install a high-gloss, joint-stabilizing wet look that protects their reputation.
  • Power washing & exterior cleaning companies – who want to turn one-time cleaning jobs into longer-term sealing and resealing work using a proven system.
  • Facility & property managers – who are responsible for walkways, plazas, pool decks, and entries and care about durability, appearance, and safety, not just today’s clean.
  • Maintenance supervisors & building engineers – who need practical, repeatable processes their crews can follow without guesswork.

Focus Products You’ll See in Action

  • Sealthane Wet Look 2-Part Urethane Sealer – a high-gloss, joint-stabilizing wet look sealer that enhances color and protects against water, stains, and UV. Under the hood, it is a 2-part, aliphatic, water-based, chemical-resistant acrylic urethane that penetrates the surface and binds joint sand.
  • PSR Gel Paver Sealer Remover – a thick, biodegradable, low-odor gel stripper that removes multiple layers of sealer and coatings without methylene chloride, N-methyl-pyrrolidone (NMP), or caustics. It uses deep-penetrating chemistry to soften built-up films so they can be removed safely and efficiently.
  • PSR Spray Paver Sealer Remover – a sprayable version of the PSR chemistry for horizontal pavers: spray on, let it dwell, and rinse to strip existing sealer and bring pavers back to a clean, resealable surface.
