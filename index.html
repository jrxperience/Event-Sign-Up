Stop Fighting Failed Paver Sealers.

Half-day, hands-on training at AZ Cleaning Supplies for landscapers, hardscapers, power washing pros, and facility teams who want a proven strip–prep–seal workflow using Inseco Sealthane Wet Look and PSR Paver Sealer Remover (gel & spray) so jobs look better and last longer with fewer callbacks.

What You’ll Learn

  • How to use Sealthane Wet Look, a 2-part, water-based acrylic urethane, to get a durable, high-gloss wet look that enhances color, binds joint sand, and stands up to water, stains, and UV.
  • How to use PSR Paver Sealer Remover (gel & spray) as your “reset button” on failed or unknown sealers – biodegradable, low-odor chemistry that penetrates and softens multiple layers so they can be removed safely and efficiently.
  • A repeatable strip–prep–seal workflow you can take back to your crews: dwell times, rinsing, surface moisture, sanding, and cure times so you know not just how to seal, but when it’s safe to seal.
  • Positioning and pricing ideas so sealing becomes a reliable, profitable service for contractors and a predictable maintenance tool for facility teams.

Who This Training Is For

This session is designed for people who actually live with the results of sealing work—not just the brochure.

  • Landscapers
    Who install or maintain pavers and concrete and want to add sealing as a high-margin add-on without creating callbacks when the sealer fails, turns white, or peels.
  • Hardscape contractors
    Who need a reliable way to strip off bad or unknown sealers, reset a surface, and then install a high-gloss, joint-stabilizing wet look finish that makes your installs pop and protects your reputation.
  • Power washing & exterior cleaning companies
    Who want to turn one-time cleaning jobs into longer-term sealing and resealing work using a proven system.
  • Facility & property managers
    Who are responsible for walkways, plazas, pool decks, and entries and care about durability, appearance, and safety – and want chemistry that works in the field, not just on paper.
  • Maintenance supervisors & building engineers
    Who need practical, repeatable processes their crews can follow without guesswork.

Focus Products You’ll See in Action

  • Sealthane Wet Look 2-Part Urethane Sealer – a 2-part, aliphatic, water-based, chemical-resistant acrylic urethane that penetrates the surface, enhances color, binds joint sand, and leaves a tough, clear high-gloss wet look film that protects against water, staining, and UV.
  • PSR Gel Paver Sealer Remover – a thick, biodegradable, low-odor gel stripper that removes multiple layers of sealer and coatings without methylene chloride, N-methyl-pyrrolidone, or caustics, designed to penetrate and soften built-up films so they can be removed safely.
  • PSR Spray Paver Sealer Remover – the same PSR chemistry in a sprayable formula for horizontal pavers: spray on, let it dwell, and rinse to strip existing sealer and bring pavers back to a clean, resealable surface.
